Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new medication reminder?
Click the "+" button in the Schedule or Reminders page, select "Medication", and fill in the details including name, dosage, and schedule.
Can I manage medications for multiple family members?
Yes! Add family members in the Family page, then assign medications and appointments to specific family members when creating reminders.
How do notifications work?
You'll receive browser notifications for upcoming medications and appointments. Make sure to allow notifications when prompted.

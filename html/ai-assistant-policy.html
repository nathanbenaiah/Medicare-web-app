AI Assistant Policy

How our AI assistant helps you manage your health safely and effectively

AI Assistant Overview

MediCare's AI Health Assistant is designed to provide personalized health insights, medication reminders, and basic health guidance. Our AI is powered by advanced machine learning algorithms and medical knowledge bases to ensure accurate and helpful responses.

Important Disclaimer

Our AI Assistant is not a replacement for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals for medical concerns.

AI Capabilities & Features

Medication Management

Smart reminders, drug interaction checks, and dosage guidance based on your prescriptions.

Health Insights

Analysis of your health patterns, trends, and personalized recommendations.

Appointment Scheduling

Intelligent scheduling based on your health needs and doctor availability.

Symptom Checking

Preliminary symptom analysis with recommendations for appropriate care levels.

Vital Monitoring

Continuous tracking and analysis of vital signs with alert systems.

Family Care

Coordinated care management for family members with shared insights.

Data Privacy & Security

Data Collection

Our AI Assistant collects and processes the following types of data:

  • Health metrics and vital signs
  • Medication adherence data
  • Symptom descriptions and health queries
  • Appointment and care preferences
  • Device usage patterns for optimization

Data Processing

All health data is processed using:

  • End-to-end encryption
  • Anonymized analysis for pattern recognition
  • Compliance with international healthcare data standards
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

Limitations & Disclaimers

Medical Limitations

  • AI cannot diagnose medical conditions
  • Not suitable for emergency medical situations
  • Cannot replace doctor-patient consultations
  • May not account for all individual health factors

Technical Limitations

  • Requires stable internet connection for optimal performance
  • Accuracy depends on quality and completeness of input data
  • May experience temporary service interruptions
  • Continuous learning may result in evolving recommendations

Emergency Situations

In case of medical emergencies, contact emergency services immediately. Do not rely on the AI Assistant for urgent medical care.

Your Rights & Controls

Data Control

You have the right to:

  • Access all data processed by our AI systems
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Delete your AI interaction history
  • Opt-out of AI recommendations at any time
  • Export your health insights and data

Customization Options

  • Adjust AI sensitivity and alert frequencies
  • Set preferences for types of health insights
  • Choose which family members can access AI insights
  • Configure emergency contact protocols

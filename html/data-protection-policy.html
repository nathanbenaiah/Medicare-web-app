Data Protection Policy

How we protect and manage your personal and health information

Data Protection Overview

At MediCare+, we are committed to protecting your personal and health information. This Data Protection Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data in compliance with Sri Lankan data protection laws and international healthcare privacy standards.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to know what data we collect, how it's used, who it's shared with, and how to control your information. This policy ensures transparency in all our data handling practices.

What Data We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name, date of birth, gender, and contact details
  • National ID number and emergency contact information
  • Insurance information and billing details
  • Login credentials and account preferences

Health Information

  • Medical history, symptoms, and diagnoses
  • Medication records and treatment plans
  • Laboratory results and medical imaging
  • Appointment history and provider notes

Technical Data

  • Device information and IP addresses
  • Usage analytics and app performance data
  • Location data (only when explicitly consented)
  • Communication logs and support interactions

How We Use Your Data

Primary Healthcare Purposes

  • Providing medical consultations and treatment recommendations
  • Facilitating communication between patients and healthcare providers
  • Managing appointments and medication schedules
  • Generating health reports and tracking progress

Platform Improvement

  • Enhancing AI diagnostic capabilities through anonymized data
  • Improving user experience and platform functionality
  • Developing new features and services
  • Ensuring platform security and preventing fraud

Legal and Regulatory Compliance

  • Meeting healthcare regulatory requirements
  • Responding to legal requests and court orders
  • Protecting against legal liability
  • Conducting internal audits and quality assessments

Data Security Measures

Technical Safeguards

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Advanced encryption at rest for stored data
  • Multi-factor authentication and access controls
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

Administrative Safeguards

  • Strict employee access controls and training programs
  • Regular security awareness training
  • Incident response procedures and breach protocols
  • Third-party security assessments and certifications

Physical Safeguards

  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring
  • Biometric access controls and surveillance systems
  • Environmental controls and backup power systems
  • Secure disposal of physical storage media

Data Security Architecture

Our multi-layered security approach includes network firewalls, intrusion detection systems, data loss prevention tools, and continuous monitoring to ensure your health information remains protected at all times.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Authorized Healthcare Providers

We share your health information with healthcare providers in our network only as necessary for your treatment, with your explicit consent or as permitted by law.

Third-Party Service Providers

Limited data may be shared with vetted service providers who help us operate the platform, such as cloud hosting, payment processing, and customer support services. All third parties are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or regulatory authority, or to protect the safety of individuals or the public health.

No Unauthorized Sharing

We never sell, rent, or share your personal health information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent. Your data is used solely for healthcare and platform improvement purposes.

Your Data Rights

Access and Portability

  • Request copies of your personal and health data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Receive data usage reports and access logs

Correction and Updates

  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Update your personal details and preferences
  • Request amendments to your health records

Deletion and Restriction

  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Restrict processing for specific purposes
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer through the platform or email us at privacy@medicare-plus.lk. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Data Retention

Retention Periods

  • Active user data: Retained while account is active
  • Health records: 7 years after last treatment (as per Sri Lankan law)
  • Billing information: 7 years for tax and audit purposes
  • Support communications: 3 years for quality assurance

Secure Deletion

When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted using industry-standard methods that ensure information cannot be recovered or reconstructed.

Legal Holds

In cases of legal proceedings or regulatory investigations, data may be retained beyond normal retention periods as required by law.

Questions About Data Protection?

Our Data Protection Officer is available to address any concerns about how we handle your personal and health information.

Help Center

Find answers to frequently asked questions about MediCare+

What is MediCare+?

MediCare+ is a comprehensive digital healthcare platform designed specifically for Sri Lankan citizens. Our platform provides:

  • Seamless appointment booking with healthcare providers
  • Smart medication reminder systems
  • Secure health data management
  • Integration with local hospitals and pharmacies
  • Real-time health monitoring and analytics
  • Family healthcare coordination

We bridge the gap between traditional healthcare and modern technology, making healthcare more accessible and manageable for everyone in Sri Lanka.

How do I book appointments?

Booking appointments through MediCare+ is simple and convenient:

  • Step 1: Navigate to the "Appointments" section in your dashboard
  • Step 2: Select your preferred hospital or clinic from our partner network
  • Step 3: Choose the department and doctor you want to consult
  • Step 4: Pick an available time slot that suits your schedule
  • Step 5: Confirm your appointment and receive instant confirmation

You'll receive automatic reminders via SMS and app notifications. You can also reschedule or cancel appointments up to 2 hours before the scheduled time.

How do medicine reminders work?

Our smart medicine reminder system helps you never miss a dose:

  • Setup: Add your medications with dosage and timing information
  • Smart Scheduling: Set custom reminder intervals (daily, weekly, or specific days)
  • Multiple Alerts: Receive notifications through app, SMS, and email
  • Dosage Tracking: Mark medications as taken or skipped
  • Refill Alerts: Get notified when it's time to refill your prescription
  • Family Coordination: Family members can help monitor medication adherence

The system learns from your habits and adjusts reminder timing for optimal effectiveness.

How is my data protected?

MediCare+ implements industry-leading security measures to protect your health data:

  • End-to-End Encryption: All data is encrypted during transmission and storage
  • Secure Cloud Infrastructure: Data stored on Supabase with enterprise-grade security
  • Access Controls: Multi-factor authentication and role-based access
  • Regular Security Audits: Continuous monitoring and vulnerability assessments
  • Data Minimization: We only collect data necessary for healthcare services
  • Compliance: Fully compliant with Sri Lanka's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA)

Your health information is never shared without your explicit consent and remains under your complete control.

Who can access my information?

Your privacy is our top priority. Access to your information is strictly controlled:

  • You: Full access to all your health data and account settings
  • Authorized Healthcare Providers: Only doctors you've given explicit consent to
  • Family Members: Limited access based on permissions you grant
  • Emergency Contacts: Basic medical information during emergencies only
  • Pharmacies: Prescription information for medication dispensing (with consent)

You can review, modify, or revoke access permissions at any time through your privacy settings. MediCare+ staff cannot access your medical records without proper authorization.

What if I forget my login details?

Don't worry! Recovering your account is quick and secure:

  • Password Reset: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
  • Email Verification: Enter your registered email address
  • Secure Link: Check your email for a password reset link
  • New Password: Create a strong, new password
  • Account Recovery: For username issues, contact support with verification details

If you can't access your email, contact our support team with your registered phone number and national ID for identity verification.

Does MediCare+ replace physical consultations?

No, MediCare+ complements but does not replace physical healthcare:

  • Enhanced Access: We facilitate easier booking and management of physical appointments
  • Telemedicine Support: Video consultations for routine follow-ups and minor issues
  • Emergency Disclaimer: Always seek immediate medical attention for emergencies
  • Diagnostic Limitations: Physical examinations and tests still require in-person visits
  • Chronic Care Management: Helps monitor ongoing conditions between doctor visits
  • Medication Management: Supports adherence to prescribed treatments

We work alongside healthcare providers to improve your overall healthcare experience, not replace essential medical care.

Is MediCare+ compliant with Sri Lankan health standards?

Yes, MediCare+ is fully compliant with Sri Lankan healthcare regulations:

  • Ministry of Health (MoH) Standards: Follows all telemedicine and eHealth guidelines
  • Digital Health Blueprint: Aligned with Sri Lanka's national digital health strategy
  • Medical Council Approval: All healthcare providers are verified and licensed
  • Data Protection Act (PDPA): Full compliance with privacy and data protection laws
  • Pharmacy Regulations: Integration follows pharmaceutical council guidelines
  • Hospital Partnerships: Only works with accredited healthcare institutions
  • Regular Audits: Continuous compliance monitoring and updates

Our platform is regularly updated to meet evolving healthcare standards and regulatory requirements in Sri Lanka.

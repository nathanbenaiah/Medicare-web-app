Help Center

Find answers to frequently asked questions

How do I create an account?
You can create an account by clicking the "Sign In / Sign Up" button and following the registration process.
How do I set medication reminders?
Go to the Reminders section, click "Add Reminder", and enter your medication details including time and frequency.
Can I book appointments through the platform?
Yes, you can book appointments with registered healthcare providers through our Appointments section.
Is my health data secure?
Yes, we use bank-level encryption and comply with international healthcare data protection standards to keep your information secure.

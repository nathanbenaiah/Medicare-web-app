Privacy Policy

How we protect and manage your personal health information

Introduction

MediCare+ ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal and medical data when you use our telemedicine platform in Sri Lanka.

Important Notice

Your health information is protected under Sri Lankan healthcare privacy laws and international standards. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

This policy complies with:

  • Personal Data Protection Act No. 9 of 2022 (Sri Lanka)
  • Telemedicine Guidelines issued by the Ministry of Health, Sri Lanka
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) standards
  • Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) principles

Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide you with personalized healthcare services:

Personal Information

Name, date of birth, contact details, emergency contacts, and identification numbers.

Health Information

Medical history, symptoms, diagnoses, medications, allergies, and treatment records.

Technical Data

Device information, IP address, usage patterns, and app performance data.

Financial Information

Payment details, insurance information, and billing addresses for transactions.

Location Data

Location information to find nearby healthcare providers and pharmacies.

Communication Data

Messages with healthcare providers, support interactions, and consultation records.

How We Use Your Information

Primary Healthcare Purposes

  • Providing medical consultations and telemedicine services
  • Managing your health records and medical history
  • Coordinating care between healthcare providers
  • Medication management and interaction checking
  • Appointment scheduling and reminders
  • Emergency medical assistance

Platform Improvement

  • Enhancing AI health assistant capabilities
  • Improving user experience and platform functionality
  • Developing new healthcare features
  • Ensuring platform security and preventing fraud
  • Analyzing usage patterns for optimization

Communication

  • Sending appointment reminders and health alerts
  • Providing customer support
  • Sharing important health and safety information
  • Sending platform updates and feature notifications

Information Sharing

We share your information only when necessary and with your consent:

Healthcare Providers

Your medical information is shared with licensed healthcare professionals providing your care, including doctors, specialists, pharmacists, and medical staff involved in your treatment.

Emergency Situations

In medical emergencies, we may share critical health information with emergency services, hospitals, and first responders to ensure you receive appropriate care.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court orders, or regulatory authorities, always ensuring minimal disclosure necessary.

Service Providers

Trusted third-party services that help us operate the platform, including:

  • Cloud storage providers (with healthcare-grade security)
  • Payment processors (PCI DSS compliant)
  • Analytics providers (with anonymized data only)
  • Communication services (encrypted messaging)

We Never

We never sell your personal health information to advertisers, marketers, or data brokers. Your health data is never used for commercial purposes outside of providing healthcare services.

Data Security

Technical Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for all health data transmission
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Multi-factor authentication for account access
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with healthcare certifications
  • Data backup and recovery systems

Administrative Security

  • Strict access controls and role-based permissions
  • Employee background checks and security training
  • Regular security policy updates
  • Incident response and breach notification procedures
  • Compliance with healthcare security standards

Physical Security

  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring
  • Biometric access controls
  • Environmental controls and fire protection
  • Redundant power and network systems

Your Rights & Choices

Access & Control

  • Access: View all personal and health information we have about you
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Export your health data in a standard format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your information

Communication Preferences

  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Choose preferred communication methods
  • Set notification preferences for health alerts
  • Control marketing and promotional messages

Data Sharing Controls

  • Choose which healthcare providers can access your records
  • Set emergency contact preferences
  • Control family member access to your information
  • Manage third-party app connections

Data Retention

Health Records

Medical records are retained for a minimum of 7 years after your last interaction, as required by Sri Lankan healthcare regulations. Critical health information may be retained longer for continuity of care.

Account Information

Personal account information is retained while your account is active and for 2 years after account closure, unless longer retention is required by law.

Communication Records

Healthcare communications are retained for 3 years for quality assurance and legal compliance. Support communications are retained for 1 year.

Anonymous Analytics

Anonymized usage data may be retained indefinitely for research and platform improvement purposes.

Children's Privacy

MediCare+ provides healthcare services for users of all ages, including children under 18.

Parental Consent

  • Parent or guardian consent required for users under 18
  • Parents can access and manage their child's health information
  • Special protections for sensitive health information
  • Age-appropriate privacy controls

Teen Privacy (16-17 years)

  • Teens can have private consultations for certain health matters
  • Reproductive health consultations may be kept confidential
  • Mental health sessions can be private with teen consent
  • Parents notified of emergency situations only

International Transfers

Your health data is primarily stored in Sri Lankan data centers. When international transfers are necessary:

  • Transfers only to countries with adequate data protection laws
  • Additional contractual protections for data security
  • Encryption during transit and at destination
  • Regular audits of international service providers
  • Right to object to international transfers

Data Localization

Critical health data remains within Sri Lanka whenever possible. International transfers are limited to non-sensitive operational data and emergency medical situations.

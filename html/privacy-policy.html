Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data protection are our highest priorities

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

Introduction

MediCare+ ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our digital healthcare platform.

By using MediCare+, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy and Sri Lanka's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).

Information We Collect

Personal Health Information

We collect the following types of personal health data to provide you with comprehensive healthcare services:

  • Identity Information: Name, date of birth, national ID number, contact details
  • Medical History: Past diagnoses, treatments, allergies, current medications
  • Appointment Data: Scheduled visits, healthcare provider information, consultation notes
  • Medication Information: Prescriptions, dosages, reminder preferences, adherence tracking
  • Health Metrics: Vital signs, lab results, diagnostic reports (when shared by you or your healthcare provider)
  • Emergency Contacts: Family members or caregivers authorized to access your information

Technical Information

  • Device information (type, operating system, browser)
  • IP address and location data (for local healthcare provider recommendations)
  • Usage patterns and platform interaction data
  • Login timestamps and security logs

Data Minimization Principle

We only collect information that is necessary to provide our healthcare services. You have control over what information you choose to share with us.

How We Store and Protect Your Data

Secure Cloud Infrastructure with Supabase

MediCare+ utilizes Supabase, a secure and compliant cloud database platform, to store your health information. Our data protection measures include:

End-to-End Encryption

All data is encrypted during transmission using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256 encryption

Access Controls

Multi-factor authentication and role-based access ensure only authorized users can access your data

Secure Servers

Data stored on enterprise-grade servers with 99.9% uptime and regular security updates

Regular Audits

Continuous security monitoring and compliance audits to maintain highest standards

Data Backup and Recovery

  • Automated daily backups with multiple geographic locations
  • Point-in-time recovery capabilities
  • Disaster recovery procedures tested regularly
  • Data integrity checks and validation processes

Who Can Access Your Information

Your health information access is strictly controlled and limited to:

You (Primary User)

  • Complete access to all your health data
  • Full control over privacy settings and permissions
  • Ability to download, modify, or delete your information

Authorized Healthcare Providers

  • Doctors and medical professionals you've explicitly granted consent to
  • Access limited to information relevant to your care
  • All access logged and auditable
  • Consent can be revoked at any time

Designated Family Members/Caregivers

  • Limited access based on permissions you grant
  • Typically includes appointment information and medication reminders
  • Access levels can be customized and modified

Partner Pharmacies (With Consent)

  • Prescription information for medication dispensing only
  • Access requires your explicit authorization
  • Limited to current prescriptions and refill information

Important Note

MediCare+ staff and administrators cannot access your medical records without proper legal authorization. Emergency access protocols exist only for life-threatening situations with appropriate documentation.

Sri Lankan Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) Compliance

MediCare+ is fully compliant with Sri Lanka's Personal Data Protection Act and follows these principles:

Lawful Processing

  • All data processing is based on your explicit consent
  • Processing necessary for healthcare service delivery
  • Compliance with legal obligations in healthcare

Data Subject Rights

  • Right to Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Right to Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Opt-out of certain data processing activities

Consent Management

  • Clear and granular consent options
  • Easy withdrawal of consent mechanisms
  • Regular consent review and reconfirmation

Data Sharing and Sale Policy

Our Commitment

MediCare+ NEVER sells, rents, or trades your personal health information. Your data is never shared for commercial purposes without your explicit permission.

When We May Share Information

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing with healthcare providers
  • Emergency Situations: Life-threatening circumstances requiring immediate medical attention
  • Legal Requirements: Court orders or regulatory demands (you will be notified unless legally prohibited)
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help operate our platform (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Anonymized Data for Research

We may use anonymized, aggregate data for healthcare research and platform improvement. This data cannot be traced back to individual users and helps improve healthcare outcomes in Sri Lanka.

Data Retention and Deletion

Retention Periods

  • Active Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Medical Records: Retained for 7 years after last interaction (as per medical standards)
  • System Logs: Retained for 2 years for security purposes
  • Marketing Communications: Until you opt-out

Data Deletion Rights

You can request deletion of your data at any time. However, some information may be retained for:

  • Legal and regulatory compliance
  • Ongoing medical care continuity
  • Security and fraud prevention

International Data Transfers

While MediCare+ operates primarily within Sri Lanka, some technical services may involve international data transfers. When this occurs:

  • We ensure adequate protection through contractual safeguards
  • Data is only transferred to countries with adequate protection levels
  • You will be informed of any international transfers affecting your data
  • Additional consent may be required for certain transfers

Your Rights and Controls

Privacy Dashboard

Access your comprehensive privacy controls through your account dashboard:

  • View and modify privacy settings
  • Manage consent preferences
  • Review data access logs
  • Download your data
  • Request data deletion

Communication Preferences

  • Choose how you receive health reminders
  • Opt-in or opt-out of marketing communications
  • Customize notification frequency and methods

Children's Privacy

MediCare+ takes special care in protecting children's health information:

  • Users under 18 require parental consent
  • Limited data collection for minors
  • Enhanced security measures for pediatric accounts
  • Special consent requirements for sharing children's health data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes:

  • You will be notified via email and platform notifications
  • Major changes require your renewed consent
  • You have 30 days to review and accept changes
  • Previous versions remain available for your reference

Privacy Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact our Data Protection Officer.

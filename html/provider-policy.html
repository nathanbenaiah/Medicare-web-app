Provider Policy

Terms, conditions, and guidelines for MediCare+ healthcare providers

Introduction

Welcome to the MediCare+ Provider Program. This Provider Policy outlines the terms, conditions, responsibilities, and benefits for healthcare providers, pharmacies, insurance companies, and technology providers who join our ecosystem.

By registering as a MediCare+ provider, you agree to comply with all terms and conditions outlined in this policy. This document serves as a binding agreement between your organization and MediCare+.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

This policy is effective from January 1, 2025, and supersedes all previous provider agreements. Regular updates may be made to reflect changes in regulations, technology, or business practices.

Provider Benefits

MediCare+ providers enjoy exclusive benefits and support to grow their healthcare practice and improve patient outcomes through our advanced technology platform.

Increased Patient Volume

Access to our growing network of patients across Sri Lanka seeking quality healthcare services.

AI-Powered Tools

Advanced AI assistants, predictive analytics, and automated workflow management systems.

Revenue Optimization

Smart pricing insights, capacity optimization, and revenue management tools.

24/7 Technical Support

Dedicated support team available around the clock to assist with technical issues.

Marketing & Branding

Co-marketing opportunities and enhanced visibility through our platform and channels.

Analytics & Insights

Comprehensive reporting and analytics to track performance and patient satisfaction.

Provider Obligations

All MediCare+ providers must adhere to specific obligations to maintain provider status and ensure high-quality patient care across our network.

Quality Standards

Maintain high standards of medical care and patient service as outlined in our quality guidelines.

License Compliance

Keep all medical licenses, certifications, and permits current and in good standing.

Data Security

Implement robust data protection measures and comply with healthcare privacy regulations.

Platform Integration

Properly integrate with MediCare+ systems and maintain accurate, up-to-date information.

Response Time

Maintain reasonable response times for patient inquiries and appointment requests.

Continuous Training

Participate in required training programs and stay updated with platform features.

Application & Approval Process

The provider application process is designed to ensure that all providers meet our quality standards and can provide excellent patient care through our platform.

Application Submission

Submit completed application form with all required documents including business registration, medical licenses, and certifications.

Initial Review

Our team conducts initial review of application and documents within 48-72 hours. Additional information may be requested if needed.

Verification & Site Visit

Verification of credentials and, if applicable, site visit to assess facilities and quality standards. This may take 1-2 weeks.

Integration Planning

Upon approval, our technical team works with you to plan integration, customize settings, and provide training.

Go Live

Launch your partnership and start serving patients through the MediCare+ platform with ongoing support and monitoring.

Financial Terms

Revenue Sharing Model

MediCare+ operates on a transparent revenue-sharing model designed to benefit both partners and the platform while maintaining affordable healthcare for patients.

  • Platform fee: 3-8% of transaction value (varies by partner type and volume)
  • Payment processing: Standard merchant rates apply
  • Monthly settlements with detailed reporting
  • No hidden fees or surprise charges

Payment Terms

Payments are processed monthly with settlements occurring within 7 business days of month-end. All payments include detailed transaction reports for reconciliation.

Special Pricing for Early Partners

Partners joining before March 31, 2025, are eligible for reduced platform fees for the first 12 months of partnership.

Data Protection & Privacy

Patient Data Protection

All partners must comply with Sri Lankan healthcare privacy laws and international best practices for patient data protection.

  • End-to-end encryption for all patient communications
  • Secure data storage with regular backups
  • Access controls and audit trails
  • Regular security assessments and updates

Data Ownership

Partners retain ownership of their patient data. MediCare+ serves as a secure custodian and facilitator, ensuring data is used only for authorized healthcare purposes.

Breach Notification

Any suspected data breach must be reported to MediCare+ within 24 hours. We will work together to investigate, contain, and remediate any security incidents.

Termination Policy

Voluntary Termination

Partners may terminate their agreement with 30 days written notice. We will assist with data export and transition to ensure minimal disruption to patient care.

Termination for Cause

MediCare+ reserves the right to terminate partnerships immediately for:

  • Violation of quality standards or patient safety
  • Loss of required licenses or certifications
  • Breach of data protection requirements
  • Fraudulent activities or misrepresentation
  • Failure to maintain platform integration

Post-Termination

Following termination, partners will have 90 days to export their data. All platform access will be revoked, and any outstanding payments will be settled according to standard terms.

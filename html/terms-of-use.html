Emergency Medical Care Disclaimer

Critical Emergency Notice

MediCare+ is NOT a replacement for emergency medical care. In case of medical emergencies, always call 1990 (Sri Lankan emergency services) or go to the nearest hospital immediately.

When to Seek Immediate Medical Attention

  • Life-threatening conditions: Heart attack, stroke, severe injuries, difficulty breathing
  • Severe symptoms: Chest pain, sudden severe headache, loss of consciousness
  • Serious accidents: Major trauma, burns, poisoning
  • Mental health crises: Suicidal thoughts, severe depression episodes
  • Pediatric emergencies: High fever in infants, severe allergic reactions

Platform Limitations

  • No real-time emergency response capabilities
  • Cannot replace physical examination by healthcare professionals
  • Limited diagnostic capabilities without physical assessment
  • Cannot provide immediate life-saving interventions
  • Telemedicine consultations are for non-emergency care only

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025 | Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

MediCare+ ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal and medical data when you use our telemedicine platform in Sri Lanka.

Our platform connects patients with licensed healthcare professionals registered with the Sri Lanka Medical Council (SLMC) and utilizes artificial intelligence to provide health insights and recommendations. However, AI suggestions are supplementary tools and do not replace professional medical diagnosis or treatment.

We strictly comply with:

  • The Personal Data Protection Act No. 9 of 2022 of Sri Lanka
  • Telemedicine Guidelines issued by the Ministry of Health, Sri Lanka
  • Medical (Amendment) Act No. 30 of 2020
  • Computer Crimes Act No. 24 of 2007
  • International data protection standards where applicable

2. What Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

a. Personal Information

  • Full Name
  • NIC or Passport Number
  • Contact Information (phone number, email)
  • Address
  • Date of Birth

b. Medical Information

  • Symptoms
  • Diagnosis
  • Prescriptions
  • Medical history
  • Consultation records
  • Uploaded medical reports or lab results

c. Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Device type
  • Browser type
  • Location data (only if you permit)

3. How We Use Your Data

We process your personal and medical information for the following lawful purposes:

a. Primary Healthcare Services

  • Facilitating consultations with SLMC-registered healthcare professionals
  • Maintaining comprehensive medical records and consultation history
  • Enabling secure communication between patients and healthcare providers
  • Processing e-prescriptions and medication orders
  • Coordinating laboratory tests and diagnostic services

b. AI-Assisted Health Insights

  • Providing preliminary health assessments and symptom analysis
  • Generating health risk predictions and wellness recommendations
  • Offering medication reminders and health tracking
  • Creating personalized health insights based on your medical history

Important: AI recommendations are supplementary tools only and do not constitute medical diagnosis, treatment, or professional medical advice.

c. Platform Operations

  • Processing payments and billing for services
  • Providing customer support and technical assistance
  • Improving platform functionality and user experience
  • Ensuring platform security and preventing fraud
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements in Sri Lanka
  • Conducting quality assurance and service improvement

4. AI Disclaimer

AI IS NOT A DOCTOR.

Our platform may use Artificial Intelligence to suggest health-related insights. These are purely assistive tools, not substitutes for licensed medical advice. Always follow your doctor's final instructions.

We are not liable for decisions made solely based on AI output.

5. Data Storage & Protection

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal and medical information:

a. Technical Safeguards

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Advanced encryption standards (AES-256) for data storage
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with regular security audits
  • Multi-factor authentication for healthcare provider access
  • Regular security vulnerability assessments and penetration testing
  • Automated backup systems with disaster recovery protocols

b. Access Controls

  • Role-based access control limiting data access to authorized personnel only
  • Healthcare providers can only access data of their assigned patients
  • Comprehensive audit logs tracking all data access and modifications
  • Regular access reviews and permission updates

c. Compliance & Standards

  • Full compliance with Personal Data Protection Act No. 9 of 2022
  • Adherence to Ministry of Health telemedicine security guidelines
  • Implementation of international healthcare data security standards
  • Regular compliance audits and certifications

6. Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell your personal data.

We only share your data with:

  • Licensed healthcare providers for your consultations
  • Pharmacy partners (with your consent) to deliver medicine
  • Laboratories (only when you request test services)
  • Government health authorities (only if legally required)

7. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • As long as your account is active
  • Or as long as necessary to comply with medical and legal standards
  • You can request deletion anytime, subject to legal retention rules.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your medical records
  • Correct incorrect information
  • Withdraw consent (unless legally restricted)
  • Request deletion of your account

9. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Maintain session security
  • Understand user behavior
  • Improve user experience

You may disable cookies via your browser settings, but this may affect platform functionality.

10. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for users under 16 without parental consent. We do not knowingly collect data from minors without permission.
